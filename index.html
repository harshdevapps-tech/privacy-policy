💰 Privacy Policy

Expense Tracker  ·  Effective: March 2026

1. Introduction

Expense Tracker is a personal finance app that helps you track your daily expenses. We take your privacy seriously and only collect what is strictly necessary to run the app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Email address — for account creation and login only
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and notes you enter yourself
  • Ad data — automatically collected by Google AdMob (see Section 4)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your personal account
  • To securely store and display your expense records
  • To show advertisements via Google AdMob

We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to any third party.

4. Google AdMob Advertising

This app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may use your device information to show relevant ads. This is fully managed by Google under their own privacy policy.

To opt out of personalized ads on Android:
Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud servers with encryption in transit and at rest. Only you can access your own expense data — no one else can see it.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete individual expenses anytime within the app. To delete your entire account and all data permanently, contact us at the email below and we will remove everything within 7 days.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
